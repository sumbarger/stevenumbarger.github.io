Outreach

Outreach is a big part of my scientific journey. I have done a lot of things to get the public interested in the field of astronomy. I have made 3D tactile models of my work to help teach individuals with visual impairments about space as well as being a mentor to high school students in research. Currently, I am running the social media team for Ann Arbor Astronomy on Tap. More details about my outreach efforts are below.

3D Tactile Models

I utilize software that converts my observational and simulation data into tactile models where light is defined by the elevation of the model.

I use these models to teach the general public about astronomy.

Science Internship Program Mentor

I mentored high school students in research.

I taught them about astronomy and prepared them for an oral presentation.

I volunteered at Lick Observatory

I helped around the observatory as well as ran the outreach table.
